Internship in the areas of microscopic urban traffic engineering and distributed traffic simulation. The project is mainly focused on data analysis of road traffic circulation with the goal of detecting and characterizing the impact of incidents in the network. My internship involved the following tasks:

  • Getting familiar with traffic simulation and particularly with SUMO simulator
  • Creation of a configurable benchmark for circulation measurements according to desired traffic conditions
  • Implementation of different routing strategies in a road network with the goal of optimizing the traffic
  • Analysis of traffic results in case of accidents in order to establish a possible correlation between static and dynamic properties of roads and the impact of accident on traffic delays

The project is mainly developed using Python.
